Generate button - generates the video (You can also just press "Enter")
Chaos mode (Beta) - generates automatically (Extremely fast by time, Ctrl+R to stop generating)
Controls (Keyboard):
Enter - Get a random video
P - Pitch mode
N - Stretch mode
"+" and "-" on numpad - Speed and slow the video (by 0.05x)

Top 1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9 - Speed by step (from 0.1x to 0.9x)
Dot on the numpad - Set video to default speed
